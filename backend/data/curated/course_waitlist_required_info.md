## Summary
Outlines information students should include in waitlist requests.

## Steps
1) Confirm your position on the waitlist.
2) Gather course details and professor contact.
3) Prepare academic justification.
4) Draft appeal message using official format.

## Required info / documents
- Course title and code
- Waitlist position
- Program or major requirements

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Missing info may reduce approval chances.
- Repeated emails may be discouraged.
