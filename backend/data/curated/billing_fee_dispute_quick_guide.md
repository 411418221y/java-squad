## Summary
How to dispute incorrect or unexpected charges on your student billing account.

## Steps
1) Review your billing statement and mark any incorrect or unfamiliar fees.
2) Collect supporting proof such as lease terms, screenshots, or prior receipts.
3) Contact the billing office through email or the official billing portal.
4) Track response timelines and follow up if no reply is received.

## Required info / documents
- Student ID number
- Billing statement showing the charge
- Proof documents (lease, receipt, screenshots)

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Some fees may only be waived if clearly proven incorrect.
- Late disputes may be denied depending on policy deadlines.
