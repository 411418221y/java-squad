## Summary
Identifies fees that typically cannot be removed or waived.

## Steps
1) Compare your charge against non-waivable fee lists.
2) Confirm fee category before submitting a dispute.
3) Contact billing only if fee appears incorrectly applied.

## Required info / documents
- Fee description
- Billing statement

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Mandatory institutional fees are often non-waivable.
- Incorrectly applied fees may still be disputed.
