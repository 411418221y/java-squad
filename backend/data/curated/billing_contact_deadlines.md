## Summary
Explains how and when to contact the billing office regarding disputes.

## Steps
1) Use the official billing portal for initial submissions.
2) Send follow-up emails if no response is received.
3) Track dispute deadlines listed in policy documents.
4) Escalate issues before deadline expiration.

## Required info / documents
- Case number (if assigned)
- Student ID
- Submitted dispute records

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Late submissions may not be reviewed.
- Portal messages are prioritized over email.
