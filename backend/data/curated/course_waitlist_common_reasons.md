## Summary
Lists common factors that strengthen waitlist appeals.

## Steps
1) Demonstrate course relevance to graduation.
2) Show schedule flexibility.
3) Explain prerequisite completion.
4) Express strong academic motivation.

## Required info / documents
- Degree plan
- Transcript or prerequisites

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Oversubscribed courses may deny all appeals.
- Priority may go to graduating students.
