## Summary
Explains who qualifies for family or partner housing.

## Steps
1) Confirm relationship eligibility requirements.
2) Review documentation guidelines.
3) Submit proof of partnership or dependents.
4) Await housing office approval.

## Required info / documents
- Proof of relationship
- Government ID
- Financial responsibility forms

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Domestic partners may require additional proof.
- Temporary relationships are usually ineligible.
