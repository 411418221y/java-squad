## Summary
Defines when students may qualify for fee waivers or charge removals.

## Steps
1) Identify the specific fee you are disputing.
2) Review eligibility conditions listed by the billing office.
3) Gather proof showing the fee meets waiver criteria.
4) Submit a waiver request through official channels.

## Required info / documents
- Student ID
- Fee description
- Proof of eligibility (contracts, screenshots, receipts)

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Some administrative fees are never waived.
- Waivers typically require documented proof.
