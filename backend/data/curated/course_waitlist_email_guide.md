## Summary
Provides structure for requesting enrollment from a waitlist.

## Steps
1) Introduce yourself politely to the professor.
2) State your interest in the course clearly.
3) Explain why the course is important to your academic goals.
4) Mention flexibility in attending sessions.
5) Thank the professor for consideration.

## Required info / documents
- Full name
- Student ID
- Course number and section
- Academic reason for enrollment

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Some departments prohibit direct appeals.
- Tone should remain respectful and concise.
