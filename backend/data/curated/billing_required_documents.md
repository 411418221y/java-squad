## Summary
Lists documents commonly required to dispute billing charges.

## Steps
1) Download your billing statement.
2) Capture screenshots of incorrect charges.
3) Locate contracts or agreements related to the fee.
4) Organize all documents before submission.

## Required info / documents
- Billing statement
- Screenshots
- Lease or service agreement
- Prior payment receipts

## FAQs / Edge cases
- Missing documents may delay review.
- Submissions without proof are often denied.
